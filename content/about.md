I'm Christopher Grainger, cofounder and CTO at [Amplified](https://amplified.ai).

In a past life, I was an innovation economist with a focus on climate change policy. My desire to integrate the text data available in patents with econometric analysis led me down a deep rabbit hole: machine learning. That led me to found a company, which then led me to learn far more about being a developer than I ever expected.

I write here — infrequently — about machine learning and data science in Elixir, with occasional forays into Rust, Nix, and the politics of technology.
